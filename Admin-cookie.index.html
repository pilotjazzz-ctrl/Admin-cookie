3. Standard Baking Method & Kitchen Gear

StepDirectivesGear Allocation
1. Wet MixCream butter and sugar. Whisk in egg and vanilla extract until fluid emulsion forms.Large bowl, wooden spoon, measuring spoons
2. Dry & Mix-insSift dry components. Fold mix-ins continuously until a thick dough canvas forms.Digital metric scales
3. ShapeWeigh out into 5 equal 200g portions. Shape upward into tall pyramids (do not flatten).Digital kitchen scales, flat trays
4. FreezeFreeze mounds on a plate for 45-60 minutes to lock dimensions and stop flatness runout.Parchment paper lines
5. BakePlace 2-3 per tray. Fire at 180°C Fan (200°C regular) for 14-16 minutes until edges are golden.2 large trays, parchment, oven gloves
6. FinishPress top additions immediately. Cool completely on the tray framework for 30 minutes.Cooling wire racks
